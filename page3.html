The Philippines is one of the best places to travel to with your family, a country with great beaches, cities, food, and people! A land with a beautiful environment that is surely worth travelling to for anyone in the world. There are a lot of countries that you may consider going to on a summer break, but the Philippines is one of the top contenders. So if you're going, you're in luck because this is a list for the top 3 places you should visit in the Philippines!

Boracay

Considered one of the best and most visited beaches in the philippines with it's white sand as well as their 5-star hotels.

Calle Crisologo, Vigan

A famous tourist spot, walk on the streets and see the old and historic Spanish architecture. As well as plenty souvenir shops in the beautiful and preserved district.

Rizal Park, Manila

Manila is highly recommended to go on a tour on, but one of it's most important historical landmarks is the Rizal Park. This site marks the execution of the national hero, Dr. Jose Rizal. It is also a home to a network of mueseums so this site is a must-go for anyone who is interested in the Philippines's history.
